Privacy Policy

Neon Crowd Runner

Last updated: June 17, 2026

Overview

Neon Crowd Runner ("the Game", "we", "our") is developed and published by Shayona. This Privacy Policy applies to all platforms on which the Game is available, including iOS (Apple App Store) and Android (Google Play Store). It explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding your data.

We do not collect your name, email, location, or any personally identifiable information. Game progress is stored locally on your device. The Game shows ads via Google AdMob, which may use a device advertising identifier to personalise ads. The Game also uses Firebase Analytics to collect anonymous gameplay data (such as level completion and session length) to help us improve the experience.

Information We Do Not Collect

Neon Crowd Runner does not collect:

  • Your name, email address, or contact details
  • Location data (GPS or otherwise)
  • Photos, camera, or microphone data
  • Health or fitness data
  • Contacts or calendar information
  • Browsing history or search history
  • Login credentials or account information

Data Stored on Your Device

The Game saves the following data locally on your device only using the device's standard local storage (UserDefaults on iOS, SharedPreferences on Android). This data never leaves your device:

  • Game progress (level reached, stars earned)
  • Coin balance and purchased items
  • Settings (sound, music, haptics, notifications)
  • Run history (crowd size, level, timestamp)
  • Daily reward and streak information

iOS This data is managed by iOS and may be included in your iCloud Backup if you have iCloud Backup enabled in Settings.

Android This data is stored in the app's private storage. It is automatically removed when you uninstall the app.

You can delete all locally stored data by uninstalling the app.

Advertising — Google AdMob

Neon Crowd Runner is free to play and is supported by advertising. We use Google AdMob (provided by Google LLC) to display ads within the Game, including banner ads, interstitial ads, app open ads, and optional rewarded ads.

AdMob uses mediation to request ads from multiple ad networks and show you the best available ad. In addition to Google's own ad network, AdMob may source ads from these mediation partners:

  • Unity Ads (provided by Unity Technologies) — mediated ad network
  • Meta Audience Network (provided by Meta Platforms, Inc.) — mediated ad network

These mediation partners may independently collect device advertising identifiers (IDFA on iOS, GAID on Android), IP address, and ad interaction data to serve and measure ads, in the same manner described below for AdMob. Your App Tracking Transparency choice (iOS) and Advertising ID opt-out (Android) apply to these mediation partners as well.

AdMob may collect and use your device's advertising identifier to serve relevant ads and measure performance:

  • iOS IDFA (Identifier for Advertisers) — only if you grant App Tracking Transparency permission
  • Android Google Advertising ID (GAID) — used to show personalised ads; can be reset or opted out in device settings
  • IP address (used to infer general location for ad targeting — not stored)
  • Ad interaction data (impressions, clicks, view duration)

iOS — App Tracking Transparency (iOS 14.5+)

On iOS 14.5 and later, the Game will ask for your permission via Apple's App Tracking Transparency prompt before your IDFA is used for personalised ads.

  • Allow: AdMob may use your IDFA to show personalised ads
  • Ask App Not to Track: AdMob will show non-personalised ads only; your IDFA is not used
You can change your tracking preference at any time in iOS Settings → Privacy & Security → Tracking.

Android — Advertising ID

On Android, AdMob uses the Google Advertising ID (GAID) to serve personalised ads. You can opt out of personalised ads or reset your Advertising ID at any time:

Go to Settings → Google → Ads (or Settings → Privacy → Ads on some devices) to opt out of ads personalisation or reset your Advertising ID.

Ad data is processed by Google and governed by Google's Privacy Policy:
policies.google.com/privacy

To opt out of personalised advertising by Google across all apps and websites, visit:
adssettings.google.com

In-App Purchases

Neon Crowd Runner offers optional in-app purchases (coin packs, Remove Ads, and the Starter Pack). All payments are processed by the platform's official store. We never see, handle, or store your payment information.

  • iOS Purchases are processed by Apple through the App Store. Governed by Apple's Privacy Policy.
  • Android Purchases are processed by Google through Google Play. Governed by Google's Privacy Policy.
  • We only receive confirmation that a purchase was completed — no payment details
  • Consumable purchases (coin packs) are used within the game
  • Non-consumable purchases (Remove Ads) are permanent and linked to your store account

Analytics — Firebase Analytics

Neon Crowd Runner uses Firebase Analytics (provided by Google LLC) to collect anonymous, aggregated data about how the Game is used. This helps us understand gameplay patterns and improve the experience.

Firebase Analytics may collect the following data:

  • App usage events (e.g. level started, level completed, game over)
  • Session length and frequency of use
  • Device model, OS version, and app version
  • Approximate country or region (derived from IP address — not stored)
  • In-app purchase events (product ID and value only — no payment details)

This data is anonymous and not linked to your identity. Firebase Analytics does not collect your name, email, precise location, or any personally identifiable information.

Analytics data is processed by Google and governed by Google's Privacy Policy. You can learn more about how Firebase uses data at: firebase.google.com/support/privacy.

Children's Privacy

Neon Crowd Runner is suitable for players of all ages. We do not knowingly collect any personal information from children under the age of 13 (or the applicable age in your jurisdiction).

Google AdMob is configured to treat all ad requests as coming from a general audience.

iOS Personalised advertising based on tracking is only enabled with the user's explicit consent via the iOS App Tracking Transparency prompt. If consent is not granted, only non-personalised ads are shown.

Android Users can opt out of personalised ads at any time via Android device settings (Settings → Google → Ads).

Data Security

All game progress data is stored locally on your device and its security is managed by your device's operating system and your personal device security settings (such as passcode, Face ID, fingerprint, or PIN). We have no access to this data.

Ad-related data transmitted to Google AdMob and analytics data transmitted to Firebase Analytics are encrypted in transit using industry-standard TLS encryption.

Your Rights

You have the following controls over data related to this Game:

  • iOS Tracking permission: Change at any time in iOS Settings → Privacy & Security → Tracking → Neon Crowd Runner
  • Android Ad personalisation: Opt out or reset Advertising ID in Settings → Google → Ads
  • Ad personalisation (all platforms): Opt out at adssettings.google.com
  • Game data: Delete all local data by uninstalling the app
  • iOS IDFA reset: Reset your advertising identifier in iOS Settings → Privacy & Security → Tracking
  • Android GAID reset: Reset your Google Advertising ID in Settings → Google → Ads → Reset advertising ID
