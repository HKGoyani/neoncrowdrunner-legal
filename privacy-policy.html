Privacy Policy

Neon Crowd Runner

Last updated: April 20, 2026

Overview

Neon Crowd Runner ("the Game", "we", "our") is developed and published by Shayona. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding your data.

We do not collect your name, email, location, or any personally identifiable information. Game progress is stored locally on your device. The Game shows ads via Google AdMob, which may use a device identifier to personalise ads with your permission.

Information We Do Not Collect

Neon Crowd Runner does not collect:

  • Your name, email address, or contact details
  • Location data (GPS or otherwise)
  • Photos, camera, or microphone data
  • Health or fitness data
  • Contacts or calendar information
  • Browsing history or search history
  • Login credentials or account information

Data Stored on Your Device

The Game saves the following data locally on your device only using Apple's standard storage (UserDefaults / SharedPreferences). This data never leaves your device:

  • Game progress (level reached, stars earned)
  • Coin balance and purchased items
  • Settings (sound, music, haptics, notifications)
  • Run history (crowd size, level, timestamp)
  • Daily reward and streak information

This data is managed by iOS and may be included in your iCloud backup if you have iCloud Backup enabled. You can delete all data by uninstalling the app.

Advertising — Google AdMob

Neon Crowd Runner is free to play and is supported by advertising. We use Google AdMob (provided by Google LLC) to display ads within the Game. AdMob may collect and use your device's advertising identifier (IDFA on iOS) to show you relevant ads and measure ad performance.

App Tracking Transparency (iOS 14.5+)

On iOS 14.5 and later, the Game will ask for your permission via Apple's App Tracking Transparency prompt before your device's advertising identifier is used for personalised ads. You may choose to allow or decline tracking:

  • Allow: AdMob may use your IDFA to show personalised ads relevant to you
  • Ask App Not to Track: AdMob will show non-personalised ads only; your IDFA is not used for tracking
You can change your tracking preference at any time in iOS Settings → Privacy & Security → Tracking.

AdMob may collect the following data for advertising purposes:

  • Device advertising identifier (IDFA) — only if you grant tracking permission
  • IP address (used to infer general location for ad targeting)
  • Ad interaction data (impressions, clicks, view duration)

This data is processed by Google and governed by Google's Privacy Policy:
policies.google.com/privacy

To opt out of personalised advertising by Google across all apps and websites, visit:
adssettings.google.com

In-App Purchases

Neon Crowd Runner offers optional in-app purchases (coin packs). All payments are processed exclusively by Apple through the App Store. We never see, handle, or store your payment information.

Apple's privacy practices for payment processing are governed by Apple's Privacy Policy at apple.com/privacy.

  • Purchases are consumable (coin packs, used within the game)
  • Payment data is handled entirely by Apple
  • We only receive confirmation that a purchase was completed

Third-Party Services

The Game integrates the following third-party services:

  • Google AdMob — advertising network. May use device identifiers to serve and measure ads. Governed by Google's Privacy Policy.
  • Apple StoreKit — in-app purchase processing. Governed by Apple's Privacy Policy.
  • Apple App Store — app distribution and review collection. Governed by Apple's Privacy Policy.

No analytics, crash reporting, or social login services are used.

Children's Privacy

Neon Crowd Runner is suitable for players of all ages. We do not knowingly collect any personal information from children under the age of 13 (or the applicable age in your jurisdiction).

Google AdMob is configured to treat all ad requests as coming from a general audience. Personalised advertising based on tracking is only enabled with the user's explicit consent via the iOS App Tracking Transparency prompt. If consent is not granted, only non-personalised ads are shown.

Data Security

All game progress data is stored locally on your device and its security is managed by your device's operating system and your personal device security settings (such as passcode, Face ID, or Touch ID). We have no access to this data.

Ad-related data transmitted to Google AdMob is encrypted in transit using industry-standard TLS encryption.

Your Rights

You have the following controls over data related to this Game:

  • Tracking permission: Change at any time in iOS Settings → Privacy & Security → Tracking → Neon Crowd Runner
  • Ad personalisation: Opt out at adssettings.google.com
  • Game data: Delete all local data by uninstalling the app
  • IDFA reset: Reset your advertising identifier in iOS Settings → Privacy & Security → Tracking

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected by an updated "Last updated" date at the top of this page. Continued use of the Game after changes are posted constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy.
